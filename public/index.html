    Aquarium Tracker

    Mobile app

    Built with React Native and Expo Go.

    Aquarium Tracker is a mobile app used to keep track of your aquarium. Within the app, you can create a profile for each of your aquariums and setup tasks that can be set for different times and dates. The app also lets you record, store, and review changes about your aquarium, such as water quality and livestock adjustments.
